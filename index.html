Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is Indian top order batsman in Indian national team. He opens for India. Currently he is captain of Indian team in all formats.

A right-handed opening batsman and an occasional right-arm off break bowler, he is widely acknowledged as one of the most outstanding batsmen of all time in limited overs cricket.

He plays for Mumbai Indians in IPL and captains the team.
